About Me

Hello,
My name is Darren Kay and I am 32 years old. I've been a Floridian my entire life and was born and raised in Seminole, Fl. Upon graduating High School, I attended the University of Central Florida where I received a Bachelor of Science in Molecular Biology and Microbiology. After graduating UCF, I did a short stint in a lab doing prostate cancer research but after one year I decided to switch gears and join my brother in his burgeoning restaurant business.


For the past 8 years of my life I've been living in Gainesville owning and operating a restaurant named The Coop. While operating the restaurant I lost track of life and became absorbed in my work and was blindly determined to succeed no matter what. Although the business did alright, my obsession for success drove me to work 60-70 hours a week every week! My passion for the restaurant industry began dwindling over the years as the long hours took a toll on my life. Although my restaurant wasn't the next chipotle all was not lost and I have gained an immense amount of valuable knowledge for which I will always be grateful.


For now, the journey of life has taken me on a new path in life and I have recently moved back to Orlando to pursue my passion for computer programming by attending a continuing education program at UCF. Prior to owning the restaurant I received my bachelor's of science degree in the field of Molecular Biology and Microbiology from UCF.


Now that I've got my weekends back and have a somewhat normal workday I enjoy rekindling my past hobbies, routines, and vacation. Spending time with my long-lost friends and reminiscing about the good times.
